### Q2: Form Validator with Real-time Feedback

Create a form with complex validation rules and real-time feedback.

**Requirements:**

- Fields: email, password, confirm password, age
- Validation rules:
    - Email: valid format
    - Password: min 8 chars, 1 uppercase, 1 number, 1 special char
    - Confirm password: must match
    - Age: between 18 and 100
- Show error messages below each field
- Disable submit until all valid
- Visual indicators (red/green borders)
